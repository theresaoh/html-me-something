About Theresa


I moved to Colorado 8 years ago to enjoy all the hiking, river-floating, and craft-beer-tasting that it has to offer. When I'm not practicing computer programming, I enjoy walking through Denver, knitting, woodturning, and cooking.

I'm incredibly self-motivated and driven by an interest in learning as much as possible. I'm a supportive team member, and I love solving problems with others. Things I have built recently are a meme-inspired twitter bot and an animal name generator for the Dumb Friends League.
